New General Service List Ver 1.2, also known as the NGSL, is a list of 2809 words that occur with very high frequency within the domain of general English. The NGSL is based on a carefully selected academic corpus of 273 million words and gives approximately 92% coverage of most texts.

NGSL 1.2 has been updated to include the following changes. Please note these changes affect the 1.01 SP wordlists. The 1.2 version of the NAWL, TSL, BSL, & FEL have been modified accordingly.
